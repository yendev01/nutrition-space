Nice to meet you!

Chinenye Mgbojikwe, RD, MPH

Registered Dietitian & Public Health Nutritionist

Hello! I'm Chinenye, a Registered Dietitian and Public Health Nutritionist dedicated to helping people like you achieve better health through practical, evidence-based nutrition.

With a background in both clinical dietetics and population health, I bring a unique, holistic perspective to nutrition care, one that connects science, and lifestyle.

Through Nutrition Space, I've had the privilege of supporting hundreds of clients with conditions such as hypertension, diabetes, ulcers, and weight management challenges. I also love collaborating with wellness brands and fitness centers to design sustainable, lifestyle-based nutrition programs that deliver lasting results.
